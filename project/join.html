Our Purpose

I served a mission on Ecuador Quito North, I returned home since 1 year and 9 months ago. I just think on all I passed through I came back home and I can't be more grateful with my Savior, as a lot of missionaries, I've been lost with the same question "Where do I need to start?" "What do I need to bring with me?".

This site is created with that purpose, to bring the neccesary things and bring hope that not everything is money and everything is possible, instead of worries I want to bring hope with plans, credits, gifts, giveaways, to help missionaries like me.

I love JesusChrist and this is my way to give back to Him the love and help I received from different persons and specifically from Him.
